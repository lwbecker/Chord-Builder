Chord Builder
Max needs help learning the chords!

Instructions:
To start the game, press the space bar.
To switch between different notes in the chord, press the left and right arrow keys.
To switch between note accidentals, press the space bar.
Once you correctly form a chord, you will hear the chord played back, a confirmation sound, and then the next chord will be loaded and playable.
